Holi - Festival of Colors

The pandora box of wholesome fun and Good natured pranks characterise the atmosphere in the colorful festival of holi. this festival is also slowly gaining popularity in the global space. holi is celebrated by throwing and applying colours on each other .The next morning is celebrated as Rangwali Holi (Dhuleti) where people smear and drench each other with colours. Water guns and water-filled balloons are often used to play and colour each other, with anyone and anyplace being considered fair game to color. Groups often carry drums and other musical instruments going from place to place singing and dancing. Throughout the day people visit family, and friends and foes come together to chat, enjoy food and drink, and partake in Holi delicacies.

Christhmas- Birth of Jesus Christ

The birth of Jesus Christ is celebrated with ecstatic merriment and wonderful joy, throughout India. The warm smell of walnut cakes can be inhaled in every household. As if captivated by a confetti of santa caps, Christmas sees the prevelance of these caps and reindeer headgear decorating the heads of civilians. Many people decorate Christhmas trees in their homes with colorful starts and hangingf balls. People also visit the church to seek the holy blessings of Christ on this auspicious festival.
